Stir-fried Tomato and Scrambled Eggs

Stir-fried tomato and scrambled eggs are a common Chinese dish, and it was also the first dish I learned how to make. However, the point is I don't eat eggs, so I had never known the taste of the dish I cooked for my family : D

More info!

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Tomatos
  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 Tbsp Water
  • 1/4 Tbsp Salt
  • 1/4 cup Oil
  • 3 Tbsp Ketchup

Instructions

  1. Cut tomatoes into small wedges
  2. Crack 4 eggs into a bowl and season with 1/4 teaspoon salt
  3. Beat eggs for a while
  4. Add 1/4 cup of oil and eggs to the wok and immediately scramble the eggs
  5. Add the tomatoes, water and ketchup
  6. Mix everything together and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes, until the tomatoes are completely softened
